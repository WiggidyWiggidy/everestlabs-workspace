# KRYO Brand Voice

## Model Brand: Eight Sleep
Mirror Eight Sleep's tone closely — premium, science-backed, outcome-first.

## Voice Principles
- **Confident, not arrogant** — state facts, don't boast
- **Outcome-first** — lead with what the customer gains, not what the product does
- **Data-driven** — use numbers wherever possible ('30 seconds', '1°C', '30 min/day')
- **Short and punchy** — no fluff, no filler words
- **Premium positioning** — never discount language, never desperate

## What KRYO Sounds Like
- 'Win back 30 minutes every morning.'
- 'Dubai tap water: 35°C. KRYO: 1°C.'
- 'Your cold plunge. Apartment-sized.'
- '30 seconds. Full alertness. No caffeine.'

## What KRYO Does NOT Sound Like
- 'Amazing product at an unbeatable price!'
- 'Try our revolutionary new shower system!'
- Exclamation marks everywhere
- Vague wellness claims without specificity

## Tone by Context
- **Ads:** Ultra short, punchy, single benefit, strong visual hook
- **Landing page:** Build desire → establish problem → introduce solution → prove it works → make offer → remove risk
- **Email:** Personal, direct, one idea per email
